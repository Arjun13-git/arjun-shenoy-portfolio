# Neural Horizon

## Product Requirements Document (PRD)

Version: 1.0

---

# Vision

Neural Horizon is a premium personal portfolio platform built to showcase the professional identity, technical expertise, research interests, engineering philosophy, and career journey of Arjun Shenoy R.

Rather than functioning as a conventional portfolio, the platform represents a long-term digital headquarters that evolves alongside its owner throughout placements, higher studies, research, internships, and professional career.

The portfolio should communicate engineering excellence through clarity, performance, thoughtful interaction, and timeless design.

---

# Mission

Build an immersive yet professional portfolio that demonstrates the ability to design and build intelligent software systems through AI, Software Engineering, and Research.

# Success Criteria

## User Experience

- Resume accessible within 10 seconds
- Projects accessible within 15 seconds
- Contact information accessible within 20 seconds
- Technical identity understood within 30 seconds

## Performance

- Accessibility = 100
- SEO = 100
- Best Practices = 100

# Design Philosophy

Performance over Flash.

Content over Decoration.

Clarity over Complexity.

Architecture over Shortcuts.

Timeless over Trendy.

Meaningful Interactions over Unnecessary Animations.

# Theme

Neural Horizon combines three visual concepts:

- AI Research Laboratory
- Ocean of Knowledge
- Neural Connections

Together these themes represent curiosity, continuous learning, exploration, research, and engineering.

# Long-Term Goal

The portfolio should remain relevant for the next 5–10 years, requiring only content updates while preserving the underlying architecture and design language.
